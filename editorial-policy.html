Editorial standards

Editorial Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026. NameForge publishes practical naming tools and guides for games, creators, brands, fantasy projects, pets, and related projects.

Original guidance

Our articles are written to help visitors make better naming decisions, not only to generate random words. Guides focus on clarity, pronunciation, memorability, audience fit, flexibility, and responsible pre-launch checks.

Generator content

Generator pages include category-specific explanations, tips, examples, FAQ sections, and related resources. The current MVP uses browser-side mock generation patterns and does not call an external AI API.

Review principles

  • Pages should have a clear purpose and enough context to be useful without advertising.
  • Navigation should make important pages easy to find from the header or footer.
  • Generated names should be treated as creative starting points, not guaranteed available names.
  • Policy pages should reflect how the site actually works at the time of publication.

Corrections and contact

If you find unclear guidance, broken navigation, or a page that should be improved, contact NameForge through the contact page. Feedback is used to improve future versions of the MVP.

NameForge keeps naming guidance practical and transparent.
