Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026. This page explains how the NameForge static MVP handles browser storage, contact messages, and advertising. It is provided for transparency and should not be treated as complete legal advice.

Information in this MVP

NameForge currently runs as a static website. Generated names are created in the browser. Favorites are stored locally in your browser using localStorage and are not synced to a NameForge server.

Contact form

If you submit the contact form, your message is sent through Formspree according to their service handling and privacy practices.

Advertising

NameForge includes Google AdSense verification and advertising code on public content pages. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to serve, limit, and measure ads. Utility pages such as contact, favorites, privacy, and terms are kept free of ad scripts to avoid placing ads near private forms or thin utility content.

Future updates

Future features may introduce analytics, accounts, saved projects, or other services. If that happens, this page should be updated before launch.
